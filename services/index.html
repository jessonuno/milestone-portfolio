THE PROCESS.

1

 Creating Ideas

I work together with clients and fellow developers to create and transform ideas into well, thought-out design specs. We discuss about branding and identity design to make sure your message is clearly delivered across your audience.

2

 Sketches & Wireframes

I get paper and pencil and start wireframing out the designs and create schematics, giving careful thought to position, size, proportions, and, most importantly, user flow. The next step is working with digital wireframing tools, Photoshop and Illustrator to finalize the wireframes to ensure a great user experience.

3

 Adding Style

It’s time to add some color, typography, and UI design patterns to the wireframes to create mood, tone, and style! I provide options using similar and different patterns so you can decide which one makes you happy!

4

 Site to Life

Now, here comes my favorite part. Using my front-end development skills to bring your sweet website to life! Whether it’s a personal site, a mobile device site, informational site, or even an e-commerce site, it’s going to work very well across all browsers and digital platforms.

5

 Time to Launch

There are a few final steps before your site comes to life. But don’t worry. From making sure you are able to manage your own site to optimizing the search engine to attract the targeted audience, you are in good hands. Once all is completed, all you need to do is sit back, relax, and enjoy the site.
